How to review

  1. Switch topics with the tabs on the left.
  2. Click any word in the transcript to jump the audio there.
  3. Each highlighted range is a proposed clip. Hover to see its title; click the card on the right to open it.
  4. Drag the orange handles at the start/end of a clip's highlight to extend or trim it (snaps to word boundaries).
  5. Edit titles by clicking the title text on a clip card.
  6. Toggle approval with the checkmark on each card. Unchecked clips are skipped.
  7. Add a new clip: click "+ New Clip" then click the START word, then the END word.
  8. Delete with the × on each card.
  9. Hit "Submit" when done. The renderer kicks off automatically.

Auto-saves every change. Safe to close the tab and come back.
